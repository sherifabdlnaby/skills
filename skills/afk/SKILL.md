# afk

I'm going away and can't answer questions or review. Work the rest of the session to completion under this contract.

## Ambiguity

- A question you'd have asked me -> take the most reversible sensible default and log it.
- Genuinely undecidable, or blocked on the ceiling → park it and move to the next piece of work.

## Return-to-computer summary

End with a summary for me returning to my computer, in this order:

1. **TL;DR**.
2. **Done** — each judgment call and the alternative not taken.
3. **Parked** — needs my decision; include your recommendation.
4. **Look at first** — what to review or unblock first.
5. If applicable, a way for me to test/try out what you built.

Remember to add this to your TODOs at the get go.
